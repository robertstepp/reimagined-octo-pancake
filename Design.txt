Data columns
	http://spdblotter.seattle.gov/wp-content/uploads/2015/01/beat-map-2.png
	https://www.projectknow.com/discover/arrests-across-america-dui/
	1
		Police Beat
		LetterNumber
		B-W (not all used) then 1-3 (every number exists for each letter)
		All beats of a letter are in the same precinct (each precinct has a multiple of 3 beats)		
	2
		CRIME_TYPE
		7 types
		Homicide, Rape, Robbery, Assault, Larceny-Theft, Motor Vehicle Theft, Burglary
	3
		CRIME_DESCRIPTION
		Same as CRIME_TYPE, except MVT says "Vehicle Theft is theft of a car, truck, motorcycle or any motor vehicle."
	4
		STAT_VALUE
		Integer number of occurrences, including 0
	5
		REPORT_DATE
		MM/DD/YY
		Reported at beginning of month until 2014, then switches to end of month
		Covers a month(?)
	6
		Sector
		Made of three beats, designed as letters B-W (not all used)
		17?
		Project Know provides names for each, but boundaries different than SPD beat map
	7
		Precinct
		5 in SPD jurisdiction: North, West, East, Southwest, South(east) (N, W, E, SW, SE)
	8
		Row_Value_ID
		Initial row number
		~27,489 total
